## Clear Problem Statement
Problem statement: Remote and hybrid employees at AcmeCo show declining engagement and inconsistent task completion when working from home, measured by low pulse survey scores and increased unplanned absences; this reduces overall productivity and business outcomes.

Key metrics to track: weekly pulse engagement score, unplanned absence incidents, task completion rate (sampled), relevant customer or business outcomes where applicable.

Proposed pilot: run a 12-week pilot with three teams across functions (approx. 45 employees) implementing the intervention and compare against matched control teams using historical and contemporaneous data.
